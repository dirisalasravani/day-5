Home

A home description, also known as a house description or real estate description, is the written part of a real estate listing that describes a home for sale or lease. It can include details like the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, square footage, recent renovations, and notable brands. It can also highlight the home's unique selling points, such as a swimming pool, big backyard, or garage.
